Welcome to R.E.A.L

Our aim

Our aim is to let people know about climate change in an innovative way. We want R.E.A.L to help you to take more interest and responsibility in your day-to-day actions which impact our environment, so that we can tackle climate change together

To know more, click here.

How R.E.A.L works

Learn: Using our "Climate Issues" page, you can educate yourself on the current isues around the globe and the threats of climate change

Act: Using our "What can I do?" page and our R.E.A.L app, you can put what you have learnt into practise by playing some of our games and complete tasks to earn points!
