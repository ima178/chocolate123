The idea behind R.E.A.L

We wanted to create something that allows you to educate yourself and learn about the effects and threats of climate change around the world in an innovative way, but also learn how you can become more environmentally friendly in your day-today life!

That's why we created R.E.A.L!

The R.E.A.L App

Our R.E.A.L app allows you to test your knowledge on climate Issues through games and quizzes! You can also complete environmentally friendly tasks, which you earn rewards for! The app also allows you to connect with friends and family for some friendly competition!

The R.E.A.L Website

We wanted to make it fun, as well as informative, for your to learn all about climate Issues. That is why our website contains easy to understand climate change content as well as providing a range of activities you can implement in your day-to-day life to tackle climate change.
